As you can see, the ruler of the entire Universe is the Dai Kaio. The next level of power is Kaioshin, who rules over a group of four galaxies. Below that is the Dai Kaio, which is the ruler of a single galaxy, which is divided in to four parts. Each part is ruled by a Kaio. Within each section are stars, planets, etc. Each planet is ruled by a Kami. On Earth, which is in the Northern section, the Kami at the beggining of Dragon Ball was Piccolo, and at the end it was Dende. I find it odd that at least twice in a row Earth was ruled by aliens. Anyway, some of the other planets in the Northern Galaxy that have major parts in Dragon Ball are Vegeta, where the Saiyans are from, Namek, where Piccolo and Dende are from, Kanassa, where Bardock got his gift to see the future, Arlia, which was destroyed by Vegeta and Nappa, and Yardratt, where Goku learned Instantaneous Movement.

The other three galaxies that weren't destroyed don't play much of a role in Dragon Ball, in fact the really important one is the Southern Galaxy, seen in Movie 8, where New Vegeta, and Broli, where located. (New Vegeta was destroyed by a comet.)
