Attacks

Explanation:
First Name = US/Dub Name *If there is no name, the attack has not been seen
Second name = Original Japan Name
Italicized Name = Translation
Bottom Name = Who Can Do It

Attacks
Kame Hame Ha Kame Hame Ha (Turtle Destruction Wave) A very strong blast of energy created by building power in your hands. It was created by Master Roshi, and was later mastered by several others.
Goku, Gohan Sr, Gohan Jr, Master Roshi, Jackie Chun,Goten, Chibi Trunks, Krillin, Cell, Majin Buu                                       

Kamayamaya (later changed to Masenko) Masenko (Demon Blast)   Somewhat like the Kame Hame Ha, but less powerful. Power is gathered in the attacker's hands, which are held over his head, then blasted at the opponent.
Gohan

Special Beam Cannon Makankosappo (Light of Death) Piccolo's trademark attack. Energy is gathered on two fingers, held on the forehead, and shot at the opponent in a screw-like beam.
Piccolo

Destructo Disc Kienzan (Circle Energy Slice) This attack is, in my opinion, one of the most practical attacks in all of Dragon Ball. It is made by raising your hand above your head and creating a very thin, almost saw-like circle of energy. It can slice through almost anything.
Krillin, Cell, Majin Buu

Power Ups
Kayo-Ken Kaio-Ken (Fist of Worlds) This attack increases your strength by approximatley 1 1/2 times, however it can be doubled or tripled or as many times as you wish, although your body may not be able to handle the strain. It was taught by Kaiou-Sama, and only mastered by Son Goku. The highest use of it was Kaio-Ken x20.
Goku

Super Saiyan Supaa Saiya-jin (Super Saiya-jin) This state of being is an extremley form of Saiya-jin. It is only released when a certain power level is reached, and an extreme amount of anger is released. It is characterized by it's blonde hair and muscles. In all of DB/Z/GT, we see 6 level of Super Saiya-jin. Here are they charecteristics:
Giji SSJ: This form was only seen in DBZ movie 4, it is a mid level between normal and SSJ level 1. While your hair is blown up like in the other forms, it is not blonde. It retains it's natural color.
Level 1: Blonde/Gold hair that is blown upwards by ki, green-yellow aura surrounds
Ultimate SSJ: Mid level between 1 and 2, it is extremly strong but also slow. Your muscle mass increases dramatically.
Level 2: Similar to level 1, the hair is longer and thinner and you are considerably stronger and faster.
Level 3: This form has huge muscles and, most noticabley, VERY long hair, kind of like Raditz's. The eye-brows disappear as well.
Level 4: Only seen in DBGT, this form has the SSJ3 long hair, but it keeps it's normal color. It's clothes are replaced with baggy pants (for some reason) ,it's body is covered in red fur, and the tail is regained. This form is reached by controlling the Golden Oozaru state, or by recieving an adequet number of Brute Rays, which is what Vegeta did.

Defensive
Ju-Jon (Fusion) This odd technique "fuses" two people of similar size together for 30 minutes. It is done by doing a dance made up of several poses and movements. The two fusers must do the dance completley symetrical, or the fusion will go wrong and will result in an imperfect, usually humorously wierd fusion. When done correctly, it results in a perfect fusion of the two people, although it usually looks more like one person than the other. Fusion can also be done by using the Potara earings. That type of fusion is permanent, however. Except in the case of Goku and Vegeta, that is.
Goku, Vegeta, Goten, Chibi Trunks, Kaiou-shin, Kibit, pretty much anyone who can either do the dance or wear the earings.
